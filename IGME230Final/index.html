AccuShot!

AccuShot is a simple game designed to help you get better at other games. Mainly, first person shooters. After you click on the button below, you'll be able to set the speed in which circles appear on screen, the size of the circles, and how many circles you want to appear before game over.
Your final result will display the ammount of circles you hit out of the total, along with how many times you missed. So shoot carefully!
